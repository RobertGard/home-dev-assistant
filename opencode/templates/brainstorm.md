Use the brainstorming skill:
1. CLARIFY: restate the problem in 1 clear sentence
2. DECOMPOSE: break into sub-problems (mind map)
3. ASSUMPTIONS: list hidden assumptions with risk assessment
4. ALTERNATIVES: generate 3+ distinct approaches with pros/cons
5. EVALUATE: score alternatives against weighted criteria
6. RECOMMEND: best approach with rationale and confidence level

Report: problem decomposition, assumptions register, alternatives matrix, weighted recommendation, next actions.
